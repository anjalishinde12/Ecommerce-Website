Frequently Asked Questions

How do I order?

To place an order, simply browse our menu, select your items, and add them to your cart. Proceed to checkout, enter your delivery information, choose a payment method, and confirm your order.

How can I cancel my reservation?

To cancel your reservation, please contact our customer service at least 24 hours in advance. You can reach us via phone or email.

How can I change the delivery address after order placement?

If you need to change your delivery address after placing an order, please contact our support team immediately. Provide your order details and the new address.

My payment is declining. What should I do?

If your payment is declining, please check your card details and ensure you have sufficient funds. You can also try a different payment method or contact your bank for further assistance.

How can I contact my delivery person?

You can contact your delivery person through the contact information provided in your order confirmation email. Alternatively, you can track your order status and contact the delivery person via our app.
